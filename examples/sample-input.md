# Fictional Sample Input: Northstar Support Desk

This is synthetic sample data for demonstration purposes only. Northstar Support Desk is a fictional company. The notes below are messy on purpose and should be treated as unverified until reviewed.

## Customer Context

- Customer: Northstar Support Desk
- Industry: B2B customer support software
- Team: Customer Operations and Support Analytics
- Audience for demo: VP Support, Director of Customer Operations, Support Analytics Manager, IT Systems Lead
- Meeting type: second discovery follow-up with possible technical demo
- Target demo goal: show how a support operations team could review feedback, spot urgent themes, and prepare follow-up actions from multiple customer-feedback sources.

## Discovery Notes

- VP Support said the team is "drowning in repeated complaints" from support tickets and app reviews.
- Director of Customer Operations wants faster weekly reporting without manually copying notes into spreadsheets.
- Analytics Manager cares about trend visibility by product area and severity.
- IT Systems Lead asked whether integrations are available, especially CRM and ticketing systems.
- There is pressure to show value quickly, but no confirmed implementation timeline.
- Customer mentioned that "leadership wants a single view," but it is unclear which teams need access.

## Product Notes

- Product can ingest CSV exports.
- Product has dashboard screens for themes, sentiment, and example comments.
- Product has a workflow screen where users can assign follow-up actions.
- Product documentation mentions API support in general terms.
- A teammate said CRM integration "should be possible," but no validated integration proof was provided.
- No security review has been completed for this customer.

## Unclear Claims

- "We can connect directly to their CRM."
- "The dashboard will save the team 10 hours every week."
- "The system can automatically classify every complaint by product area."
- "Implementation is straightforward and should only take a few days."
- "This can replace their weekly reporting process."

## Risky Assumptions

- Customer's CRM is supported.
- Ticket exports contain clean enough data.
- The VP Support has budget authority.
- The app review source has usable fields.
- Product-area classification is accurate enough for leadership reporting.
- IT will approve data movement without a security review.
